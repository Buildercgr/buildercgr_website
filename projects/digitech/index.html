Digitech Mod

Buildercgr | Feb 16, 2026 min read

We’ve paused the Digitech project because we’re converting it to Java code. Thank you very much for trying Digitech, but we’ll be working on re-coding Digitech for a while to keep it more stable, funnier and with more features.

Mcreator page: https://mcreator.net/modification/110176/mobilecraft

Curseforge page: https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/digitech

Do you want mobile phones and computers in Minecraft?

With Digitech, now you can enjoy both!

This mod is still in beta, and there’s much more to come. But first, let’s take a look at the essential features:

Main elements:

Digitech adds mobile phones and PCs with lots of useful functions.

You can use menus, open apps, customize your device, and more.

I hope you enjoy it a lot — and don’t confuse this mod with others, this one’s unique!

Available for:

Forge 1.20.1

NeoForge 1.21.1 / 1.21.4

Mobile and PC features

The Menu

When you right-click your device, the main menu appears.

You can choose different apps here — and I’m planning to add even more soon!

The Functions:

Settings:

Right now, you can change your gamemode from here. More options are coming!

First Aid:

This feature can save your Minecraft life.

When you’re in danger, tap it — you’ll get an ender pearl and a totem of undying to escape quickly.

Wiki:

My favorite feature!

This adds a built-in wiki, available in English and Spanish (my language).

I’m also planning to release a separate mod to add more languages soon.

Shop:

A portable shop where you can buy items.

If you like mods that improve the Minecraft economy, this is also for you.

Ender Chest:

A secure place to store your valuable items — like a portable backpack!

Customization

You can customize your phones with cases and wallpapers.
